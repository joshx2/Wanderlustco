Hawaii Volcanoes National Park

Great place to hike and tour. Amazing sightseeing excursions that include active volcanoes, rocky craters, and hidden caves.

Hapuna Beach State Recreation Area

Perfect for that tropical, white- sand beach view that is great for the whole family. The beach is full of silky sand, clear turquoise water, and beautiful palm trees. Great place for snorkeling, kayaking, and paddle boarding.

Akaka Falls State Park

Great for hiking and exploring in the tropical rainforest. This park has numerous beautiful waterfalls, gorges, and a ton of flora and fauna. Immerse yourself in the tranquil serenity of nature and spend the day hiking, swimming, and having picnics.

Hilo

Hilo is a charming town surrounded by many natural sights. Hilo is a popular tourist destination for visitors to Hawaii. Check out the historic buildings including the Palace Theater, as well as the art galleries and local farmers market.
