Welcome to Jamaica

  • Jamaica is a beautiful country located in the Caribbean. It is known for beautiful beaches, warm climate, and amazing culture.

  • Some things you should know before visiting Jamaica:

  • The country's currency is the Jamaican Dollar

  • This one should be obvious but be aware of your surroundings!

  • Jamaica has a tropical climate so it is usually warm and sunny. So make sure to pack light!
